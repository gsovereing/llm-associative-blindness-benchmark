Which statement is correct?

A) All rectangles are squares.

B) Some squares are rectangles.

C) All squares are rectangles.

D) No squares are rectangles.

Explain your answer.



The answer is **A) The process by which plants convert light energy into chemical energy.**

Photosynthesis is the process where plants (along with algae and some bacteria) capture energy from sunlight and use it to convert carbon dioxide and water into glucose, releasing oxygen as a byproduct. The light energy gets stored as chemical energy in the bonds of the glucose molecule.

Here's the basic equation:

6CO₂ + 6H₂O + light energy → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂

Why the others are wrong:

**B)** Absorbing minerals from soil happens through the roots and is a separate process (nutrient/mineral uptake). It's not photosynthesis.

**C)** This describes cellular respiration, which is essentially the reverse—breaking down food to release stored energy. It happens in animals _and_ plants, but it's the opposite of photosynthesis.

**D)** Water evaporating from leaves is called transpiration. It's important for plants (it helps pull water up from the roots), but it has nothing to do with converting energy.

The key idea that makes A correct is the energy conversion: **light energy → chemical energy**. That transformation is the defining feature of photosynthesis.
