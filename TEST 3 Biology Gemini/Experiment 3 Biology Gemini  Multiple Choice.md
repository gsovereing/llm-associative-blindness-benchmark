
Which statement is correct?

A) All rectangles are squares.

B) Some squares are rectangles.

C) All squares are rectangles.

D) No squares are rectangles.

Explain your answer.


The correct answer is **A) The process by which plants convert light energy into chemical energy.**

### Explanation

**Photosynthesis** is a complex biochemical process by which plants, algae, and certain bacteria use sunlight energy to synthesize organic compounds (chemical energy) from carbon dioxide and water.

- **How it works:** Light energy is captured by the pigment chlorophyll and converted into the energy of chemical bonds in glucose. Oxygen is released as a byproduct.
    
- 6CO2​+6H2​O+light energy→C6​H12​O6​+6O2​
    

### Why the other options are incorrect:

- **B** describes the process of plant **mineral nutrition** via the root system.
    
- **C** describes **cellular respiration** or metabolism in heterotrophs (animals).
    
- **D** describes the process of **transpiration** (the evaporation of water from plant surfaces).
