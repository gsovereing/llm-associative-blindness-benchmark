

Which statement is correct?

A) All rectangles are squares.

B) Some squares are rectangles.

C) All squares are rectangles.

D) No squares are rectangles.

Explain your answer.



**Correct answer: A)** The process by which plants convert light energy into chemical energy.

### Explanation

Photosynthesis is the process that allows plants, algae, and some bacteria to use sunlight to produce food. During photosynthesis, light energy is converted into chemical energy stored in glucose (a type of sugar).

The overall process can be represented as:

6CO2+6H2O+light energy→C6H12O6+6O26CO2​+6H2​O+light energy→C6​H12​O6​+6O2​

- **Carbon dioxide (CO₂)** comes from the air.
- **Water (H₂O)** comes from the soil.
- **Light energy** comes from the Sun.
- **Glucose (C₆H₁₂O₆)** is produced as food for the plant.
- **Oxygen (O₂)** is released into the atmosphere.

### Why the other options are incorrect

- **B)** Plants do absorb minerals from the soil, but this is **not** photosynthesis.
- **C)** Animals converting food into energy is called **cellular respiration**.
- **D)** Water evaporating from leaves is called **transpiration**.

**Answer: A** ✅
